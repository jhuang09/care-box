Ever need a personalized box of messages to get you through the day?

We get it. It's hard juggling everything.

You already have so much on your plate.

And sometimes it's just hard to find the motivation you need.

This box is filled with inspirational notes to help you get through the days when it seems so hard to continue.

We cater every message to your needs in the hopes that they're there whenever you need them. Some may contain illustrations from our very own employees. With each box, we also include many trinkets and extras that may help reduce the stress of everyday life.

So how does this work?

  • Fill out our questionnaire so we can see what sorts of things you like and don't like!
  • Specify how you want to hold your individualized messages: jar, box, chest, bottle, bag. (We can offer other options if you need 'em. :))
  • Select from a range of items that you would like as extras/freebies. We will pack as much as we can, according to your questionnaire.
  • Wait for your care package to arrive!
